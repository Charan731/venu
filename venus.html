Types of Universities

  • Public
  • Private
  • Online
  • Community

Public Universities

Public universities are funded by the government and usually offer lower tuition fees.

Private Universities

Private universities are funded by private organizations and tend to be more expensive.

Online Universities

Online universities offer flexible learning options through virtual platforms.

Community Colleges

Community colleges provide two-year degrees and vocational training programs.
